Film

I am a lifelong cinephile and movie lover. When the British Film Institute published the results of their 2022 Sight & Sound Greatest Films poll — the most comprehensive survey of critical opinion in cinema, with over 2,000 voters — I wanted a better way to explore the data than a static list.

The result is an interactive Shiny app built in R, covering all 21,000+ ballot entries across 4,500+ unique films. It lets you browse the full rankings, explore individual voter ballots, filter by decade, country, or genre, and even build your own top-10 list and find your closest taste twin among the real voters.

The app was also an exercise in what is possible with R and Shiny for data visualisation. I built it with assistance from Claude, and the full source code is available inside the app itself — so if you want to build something similar, you can use it as a starting point.
